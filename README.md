## üìò Project Overview

**CRM for Jewel Management** is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management system built using **Salesforce Developer Account**. The system aims to simplify jewelry business operations by automating sales, inventory, customer handling, billing, and reporting within a unified CRM environment.

This project leverages Salesforce's declarative tools and Apex programming to enhance operational efficiency and ensure a seamless experience for both customers and administrators.

* To integrate **reports and dashboards** for business insights and performance monitoring.
* To reduce manual dependency through workflow automation and cloud accessibility.
* To improve customer engagement with a data-driven CRM solution.

## üß© Modules Implemented

1. **Customer Management**

   * Capture and manage customer profiles and purchase history.
   * Maintain contact details and loyalty information.

2. **Product & Inventory Management**

   * Store jewelry product details, including type, weight, price, and availability.
   * Track stock and supplier data efficiently.

3. **Sales & Billing Management**

   * Record transactions, generate invoices, and manage quotations.
   * Maintain billing history and payment information.

4. **Employee Management**

   * Assign roles, permissions, and manage user access within Salesforce.
   * Track employee sales performance.

5. **Reports & Dashboards**

   * Visualize key data such as top-selling items, revenue trends, and customer demographics.
   * Export reports for business analysis and decision-making.
